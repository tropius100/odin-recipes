Masala Tea

Description:

Masala Tea is an exquisite yet refreshing drink which gives the mind a calming effect and makes you feel relaxed

Ingredients

  • Tea powder
  • Ginger
  • Cardomom
  • Clove
  • Indian Bay leaf
  • Sugar
  • Milk

Instructions

  1. Measure 3/4th cup water and set it to boil in a cooking bowl
  2. Add 2tsp Tea powder
  3. Smash 1/2" Ginger and add it to the bowl
  4. Add 1x Cardomom, Clove and Bay leaf each
  5. Measure 3/4th cup milk and add it to the bowl
  6. Add 1 tsp sugar
  7. Cook for 2-3 minutes on medium flame
  8. Turn off the gas when the tea froth appears golden in colour
  9. Strain the tea in your cup and enjoy!
